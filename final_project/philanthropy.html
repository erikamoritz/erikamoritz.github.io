Alpha Phi Philanthropy - Women's Cardiac Care
"Through the Alpha Phi Foundation, some of the values we celebrate are: improving women’s heart health, supporting sisters in need and educating about the value of philanthropy.''
The Red Dress Gala is the largest philanthropy event held worldwide for Alpha Phi. Last year the Saint Joseph's University chapter raised $19,000 for the Alpha Phi Foundation.
The event was held at the Bellevue Hotel. With over 300 guests in attendence the night was filled with amazing h'ordeuvres, 35 baskets, 50 silent auction items, a gourmet meal, and a night of dancing.
